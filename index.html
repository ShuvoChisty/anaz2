Services

PSYCOLOGICAL ASSESSMENT

Our expert psychiatrist and psychologist go through an assessment through which they will find out the cognitive ability, behavioural and emotional aspects, and environmental adaptability of a child with special needs.

SPECIAL EDUCATION

We know, when a child has unique needs, his/her learning must be accommodated in a unique way. Children have diversified needs such as communicational, environmental adaptability, emotional and academic. Unlike others, children with special needs find it very difficult to express their needs. Therefore, our special educators assess and make Individualized Education Plan(IEP) for them. Through effective intervention, our educators help these children to become independent in their day to day life.

SPEECH & LANGUAGE THERAPY

Speech is an integral part of our life. Our speech therapist helps the children with special needs to interact, form social relationships, and function in their every day of life.

NUTRITIONIST

Children with special needs often face obstacles to have a healthy relationship with food. There could be various reasons for which a child can be over or underweight. Our nutritionist helps a child to set up specific guidelines to tackle the challenges they are facing in terms of eating food or to support a child in developing self restraints from having too much food.

PHYSIOTHERAPY

Our therapist assess and help children with movement disorders, disability or illness. Through effective intervention and support children are able to reach their full potential to function in their life.

OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY

In the life of a special needs child, occupational therapy plays a very vital role. Through effective therapeutic and sensory integration, our therapists help a child to develop motor and life skills. The aim of this is to have meaningful everyday activities that can give a child a sense of direction and purpose in life.
